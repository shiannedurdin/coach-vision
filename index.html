Blindness Is A Different Kind of Vision

Description

As a child, I was fortunate to have attended a blind school where I received support in various aspects of life with low vision such as assistive technology, braille courses and adaptive sports, however, I always lacked the comfort of having people I could talk to, who had the lived experience of a blind or low vision individual. And that is why as a support coach for the vision impaired, I not only offer personalised braille and screen reader training, tailored advice on pathways for the future, and strategies for a more independent daily life, I also offer mentorship. A space of understanding. A place where each and every one of my clients feels heard and validated. Because while blindness might mean lack of sight, it does not mean lack of vision.

Online platform Zoom will be used for all appointments and programs, all of which are designed to cater to blind and low vision individuals, as well as parents and allies.

Programs

  • 6 week introduction to navigating a computer using Jaws

    One-on-one online program. Weekly 1 hour sessions. Total cost: AUD $260 (due before the beginning of the second session).

    Topics include navigating File Explorer and Word documents, creating and saving files, connecting to internet and Bluetooth devices, configuring settings, accessing the internet via Google Chrome, Jaws-specific shortcuts, and anything the client would like to learn.

  • 6 week introduction to using iPad and iPhone with VoiceOver

    One-on-one online program. Weekly 1 hour sessions. Total cost: AUD $280 (due before the beginning of the third session).

    Topics include sending and replying to emails and messages, making calls, configuring settings, navigating websites and well-known apps, navigating documents, and anything the client would like to learn.

  • 8 week Exploring Pathways

    One-on-one online program. Weekly 1 hour sessions. Total cost: AUD $260 (due by the beginning of the third session).

    Topics include daily living skills, goal setting, peer mentoring, and emotional support. The first session explores your current, short-term, and long-term goals as an individual with blindness or low vision. Every appointment begins with a check-in on what you feel you need for the session.

  • 4 week introduction to Basic Braille

    One-on-one online program. Weekly 45 minute sessions. Total cost: AUD $230 (due before the beginning of the second session).

    Topics include the braille alphabet, basic Maths symbols, and anything else the client would like to learn.

    Braille paper and a braille machine are required for this program. Additional learning resources will be sent via post.

  • 6 Week Introduction To Braille Music

    One-on-one online program. Weekly 45 minute sessions. Total cost: AUD $320 (due before the beginning of the second session).

    Topics include basic notation and rhythm, writing scales in braille, composing simple melodies, and anything the client would like to learn.

    Braille paper and a braille machine are required for this program. Additional learning resources will be sent via post.

  • 6 Week Intermediate iPad and iPhone Navigation Using VoiceOver

    One-on-one online program. Total cost: AUD $290 (due before the beginning of the second session).

    Topics include VoiceOver shortcuts, downloading attachments within emails and messages, sending emails and messages with attachments, installing and using third party apps Be My Eyes and Seeing AI, using braille screen input mode (typing on screen using braille entry), and anything the client would like to learn.

  • 6 Week Intermediate Braille Music

    One-on-one online program. Weekly 1 hour sessions. Total cost: AUD $350 (due before the beginning of the third session).

    Topics include writing chords in braille, composing piano music for left and right hands, key and time signatures, syncopated rhythms, how to set out a piano score, repeats, dynamic markings, and anything the client would like to learn.

    Braille paper and a braille machine are required for this program. Additional learning resources will be sent via post.

  • 6 Week Intermediate Braille

    One-on-one online program. Weekly 1 hour sessions. Total cost: AUD $310 (due before the beginning of the second session).

    Topics include contractions (single combinations of dots used to write whole words), punctuation markings, intermediate Maths symbols, a brief history of braille, and anything the client would like to learn.

    A braille machine and braille paper are required for this program. Additional learning resources will be sent via post.

Flexibility: Every program can be modified to meet the needs of the client.

If you have any other topics you may wish to discuss when it comes to adapting to life with low vision such as sports for the blind, orientation and mobility, services that can assist with daily independence etc. you can also book individual sessions seperate to any of these programs.

Note: The information in this heading only applies if you are booking an individual session, seperate from the programs listed above.
